Detect Emotion

This Emotion Detector web app detects your current mood. This can be made Personalised or General depending upon user's choice. The user may press the 'Emotion' button and detect their emotion without displaying their name or can go to the 'Emotion with Name' button and fill the attached HTML form uploading their name and image. This feature displays the name of the user along with their emotion.
